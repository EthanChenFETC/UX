Ultimate tailor-made ETC solution for different levels of requirement

Since its inception, Taiwan’s nationwide Electronic Toll Collection (ETC) System has proven to be the most advanced system in the world. From the experience and expertise gained from designing, constructing, and operating Taiwan’s ETC system, FETC has designed the ultimate solution for implementing ETC systems around the world.

From ETC 1.0, highly economical single lane barrier solution through to ETC 4.0, the most advanced and high performance Multi-Lane Free Flow (MLFF) solution, FETC provides tailor made solutions responsive to specific customer requirements. Each solution is open to customization through the use of open standard integration of modular components, allowing the all customer specific requirements to be met.

Descriptions

The ETC driver enters the toll plaza at slow and stops. The driver pays the toll by touching a pre-paid contactless smart card against toll gate reader. When the toll is paid the barrier gate opens allowing the driver to exit the tolling area.

The drivers enter the toll plaza at slow speed. The DSRC/RFID reader detects the OBU/Tag and matches the driver to a pre-registered account. Toll is deducted from the drivers account when there is sufficient balance in the account. Barrier gate opens allowing the driver to exit the tolling area.

The drivers enter the toll plaza at normal driving speed without stopping. The reader detects the OBU/Tag as the driver passes. The toll is deducted from the drivers account.

The drivers enter the toll area at high speed without stopping. The reader detects the OBU/Tag as the driver passes. The toll is deducted from the drivers account.
