Vietnam

Project Name : Basic Design for Vietnam ETC System
Country/Region : Vietnam
Category : Consulting and Proof of Concept (POC)
Customer : VETC

Description:

VETC was appointed by Ministry of Transport of Vietnam (“MOT”) to carry out the project of provision of the systems of Electronic Toll Collection – ETC and vehicle load control on National Highway No. 1 and National Highway No. 14 ( Ho Chi Minh Trail – section through Central Highlands), and the VETC intends to establish Electronic Toll Collection system nation-wide.
Scope of work :

The FETC provide consultant services on establishment and operation the ETC system and other related service as follows :

Conduct the current state analysis for tolling situation of Vietnam based on the current status data provided by the FETC.
Provide the best case study for international tolling solution.
Conduct the gap analysis between current tolling status of Vietnam with the future state to identify the solution for solving the existing challenges of tolling system in Vietnam.
Propose the high level business model and operation model for the new ETC system.
Recommend the suitable technical solutions of the new ETC system.
Basic design of the high level end-end system architecture for the new ETC system.
Develop the guideline for detail design of the new ETC system.
Study and propose human resource planning for the entire system.
Study and propose legal framework and recommend solutions to optimize the operation of the system.
Propose training program and materials for human resource of the system.
Propose overall documents regarding communication for the project.
Propose, design and construct POF site in Quang Binh, Vietnam.
