Company Vision

To achieve the ultimate goal of offering a better and more efficient traffic environment to the public, FETC has set its vision as “improving traffic flow, intelligent transportation, and improved quality of life”. We are also committed to creating a win-win situation for the society, the government and the corporation.

Improve traffic flow

ETC is designed to provide the convenient services by eliminating toll booth intermediaries so that drivers do not have to stop the car to pay toll which saves tremendous amount of time.

Intelligent transportation

According to freeway usage analysis, ETC provides the most detailed, comprehensive and immediate information to improve safety and efficiency by using advanced technology, i.e., intelligent transportation system (ITS).

Improved quality of life

We regard the ETC service and intelligent transportation system (ITS) as the core of our business. To improve the quality of life, we actively develop parallel businesses to make it available for everyone.
