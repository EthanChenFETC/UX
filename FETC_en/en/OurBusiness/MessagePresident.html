Message from the President

YC Chang

President of the Far Eastern Electronic Toll Collection Co. (FETC)

Creating the win-win-win transportation era for society, government and the company.

As a member  of the national ITS ( Intelligent transportation system) and traffic development policies, Far Eastern Electronic Toll Collection Co. (FETC) was officially commissioned by the Freeway Bureau, MOTC to set up the Electronic Toll Collection (ETC) service on all freeways in Taiwan. FETC is responsible for the construction and operation of toll collection system until 2025. We are happy to dedicate our efforts to freeway users and provide a working platform for future industrial development in the field of transportation. Eventually, it is our goal to create the win-win-win for the society, government and corporation.

We launched the ETC service from February 10, 2006. Since then, ETC has experienced many difficulties and challenges. However, as a result of continuous efforts and team works, FETC overcame many obstacles and steadily gained confidence of customers and Freeway Bureau, MOTC. We are proud to be steadily heading toward the best ETC services.

“Solution and Operation” rendered by FETC has 3 leading characteristics:

The largest starting operational scale in the world: ETC service was provided on all freeways to all types of vehicles.

Successful technology integration in the world of “IC Intelligence Card” and OBU.

The first two systems model in the world: combing both DSRC and VPS system in the world. And, we expect two system approach will promote the cooperation within different industries and technological development.

ETC project is not a traditional transportation administration or traffic flow control management system, it is an important “public service”. As such, we must have advanced prospects, strategies and operations. It is not only our objective to maintain the “Three Number ONEs in the World” but cling to the following principles: be contingent, be truthful, be innovative, and “customer first”, which all serve as the core corporate values. In addition, being innovative in internal and external aspects will increase the public confidence and expectation in ETC service. The way FETC runs business in providing services to individual customers is to gather all information from the prospective of customers and place customers in the first priority in any decision making. July 2007 proved to be a significant milestone for FETC as the accumulated use of ETC lanes has reached 100 million.. As we celebrate this remarkable accomplishment, we still find rooms for further service improvement.

To gain more public confidence in ETC service, first of all we are prepared to handle any contingency and to respond to our customers any minute with well restructured plans. Secondly, one of the basic requirements we must meet is to achieve 99.98% accuracy of toll collection, which we always do. Meeting all KPI requirements is the most important principle for us. Lastly, “Three Number ONEs in the World” is not only an innovation but a creation. Pursuing innovation is also the strategic approach for all solution.

We value all customer responses and reactions from various sources. And we intergrade those concerns into every action the company takes. FETC are committed to the principle of “Customer First” and make continuous progress with tremendous amount of time and efforts to achieve highest quality of service. It is also the target that our management team has been trying to achieve.

Well-structured solution, excellent management team as well as the core value and vision of FETC are all the core capability for us to success.  FETC's operation of the ETC system can stimulate domestic development for various industries such as manufacture, optoelectronic, information, telecommunication and financial institutions. eTag offers the public with IT transportation convenience so that everyone is able to enjoy a high-quality of life with advanced technology.

Your concerns are always important to us because that motivates us to achieve the best performance. We strive to reach our ultimate goals which are to create the win-win-win situation for society, government and the company, and we always need your continued support and ongoing interests to fulfill the accomplishment.
